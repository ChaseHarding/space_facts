Welcome to Space Facts

How to Play:

On the next screen, youll be asked to press enter, afterwards a short timer will run. Once that timer finishes, you will have 10 seconds to answer the question. After the timer runs out, the answer will be revealed, if you're correct, you'll gain a point. If not, we have more questions, don't worry.
